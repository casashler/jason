Find answers to common questions or contact our support team for personalized assistance. We're always happy to help!

Booking a Stay

How do I book a property?

Search for your destination, choose your perfect stay, select your dates, and click "Book Now" to send a request to the host.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes, go to 'My Trips' to manage your bookings. Cancellation and modification policies may vary based on the host's rules.

Hosting Your Home

How do I list my property?

Sign in to your account, click "Become a Host," and follow the easy steps to create your listing.

When do I get paid?

Payments are released 24 hours after the guest checks in. You can choose from multiple payout methods!

Account & Security

How do I update my profile information?

Go to your account settings to edit your personal details, update your photo, and verify your email or phone number.

What should I do if I forgot my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the steps to reset it via email.
